About Me

Hello! My name is Daniel Roodman, and I’m 12 years old. I got inspired by my friend to learn HTML and make my very own website where I can share and sell second-hand items like golf clubs and mini-golf sets.

I made this website because I wanted to create something fun and useful. My goal is to make a simple, easy-to-use online store, and maybe one day turn it into a bigger project or company!

Even though I don’t use Instagram or Facebook (I’m just a kid!), you can check out my YouTube channel for more fun projects I work on.

Thanks for visiting my website! I hope you enjoy browsing the store and seeing what I’ve built so far.
